HOW TO GET STARTED

Get donations for your cause

Sign up your cause: every cause is welcome!

You can sign up your children's school, your local church, even your own personal challenge or your favourite community group. Every cause is a good cause

Get donations through online shopping.

Invite people to join easy fundraising and support your cause. They can then shop online with more than 7,000 brands which will donate part of their total spend to your cause as a thank you for shopping with them.

Raise even more.

Access your dashboard to check how many supporters and donations your cause gets. Boost your fundraising through our browser extension and app.

Every time anyone shops through Shopnate they collect a free donation for the charity or good cause they support. What's more is Shopnate provides numerous deals and coupons so the shoppers get to save as well. They Shop, They Save, and We Give to your cause. Over 900 Australian causes like yours are raising money everyday, simply by encouraging their friends, family, community and supporters to buy everything through Shopnate. Why not yours?

How to get started raising free funds for your good cause

Getting started is simple.

Register online
Using our simple online form, it takes less than 5 minutes to complete.
Introduce your organisation, staff and supporters
Collect the ready to use marketing resources such as our emails, images and social media assets. Use these to share Shopnate with your staff and supporters. We will also provide you with a guides, an account manager and emails to help you spread the word to your supporters so they can donate more to you.
Raise free donations.
We pay you monthly with any funds your supporters have raised for your good cause. You can also log in to your admin section at any time and see the amounts raised.
